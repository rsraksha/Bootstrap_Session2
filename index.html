Welcome to Nature in Mind

We help people improve their health and well-being through mindful engagement with nature
Nature in mind is an educational organization that aims to help people improve their health and well-being through mindfully
connecting with the natural world. We run workshops, courses, tours, and retreats in which we use a mindfulness-based approach to
explore the beauty of the natural world.
